Get Started

Use AI Accounting Assistant to record a transaction in plain English — e.g. "Purchased 5 Dell laptops for ₹50,000" — and the correct line item in the master template (AI_Financial_Report_Template.xlsx) is updated automatically. Once two quarters exist for a fiscal year, the system builds the H1 (6 months) view; once three quarters exist, the 9M view; once all four exist, the full Annual view — each with QoQ% and YoY% growth on the Profit & Loss statement. View them all under Reports.
